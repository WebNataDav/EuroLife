A Hungarian passport is among the most desirable passports in the world, offering visa-free travel within Europe as well as to the United States and Canada, among many other destinations. Hungary is a landlocked Central European country, and a member of the European Union. It is located on the Pannonian plain and Its over 93,028 sq. m. are inhabited by almost 10 million people. Its capital and most populated city is Budapest, as well as its main industrial, trade and transport center. Considered to be one of the most beautiful cities in Europe, Budapest has several World Heritage Sites, including, on the banks of the Danube, the Buda Castle district, Andrássy Avenue, the Heroes’ Square and its Metro, the second oldest in the world. Its official language is Hungarian and its legal tender currency is the Hungarian Florint (HUF), and for now, there is no set date for for the adoption of the euro.
