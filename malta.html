Apply For Your Malta Citizenship Today!

The country is a group of seven islands in the Mediterranean Sea. The capital of Malta is Valletta, which is the smallest national capital in the European Union and it is one of the smallest nations of Europe with a population of 400,000 and it is a melting pot of civilisations with a history stretching back thousands of years. Malta tax system is very favourable, official language is Maltese with Arabic roots but English is the second official language in the country. A Maltese Passport allows applicants the right to live, work and study in the 28 EU countries, and enjoy travel freedom with visa-free access to 171 countries worldwide. Through the bilateral agreements between Malta and other countries, and through Malta's traditional economic links, the country has a high position as a business location and financial centre. The Maltese passport is currently ranked as the 4th strongest passport in the world with visa free travel to 171 countries.

Programs that we offer

MIIP – CITIZENSHIP

MIIP – Maltese Immigration by Investment Program

  • Grants lifetime citizenship
  • Can be inherited
  • Best value Passport
  • Ranked 4th best globally
  • Visa-free access to 171 countries (E-visa to US and Canada)
  • Short processing time (Residency card 2 months after submission, passport 13 months after submission).

MRVP

MRVP – MALTESE RESIDENCY AND VISA PROGRAM

  • Permanent Residency for the entire family
  • Grants visa-free access to ALL European countries
  • Grants the right to work, live, start a business in Malta
  • Children up to the age of 18 can be included

GRP

GRP – GLOBAL RESIDENCY PROGRAM (temporary residency)

  • Cheapest residency option in EU and Schengen
  • Children may be included up to the age of 26
  • Parents may be included above the age of 55
  • Both EU and NON-EU citizens can aply
  • Grants Global Tax Residency Certificate
